Building this scene has a lot of suprises.
1. we are looking at the scene from the back.
2. To place the sprites in 3D space we simply use sprite3D but to place the text in the speech bubble was a lot more difficult. I ended up using sprite3Ds and a viewport for the 2D text.
3. The base and the sidewalk and street are 3D images. I change this back to 2D as it forces the camera high and to be pointed slightly down. This messes the way the characters look. I could adjust the 2d characters to be at the same angle as the camera (with code), but this requires extra care when flipping the camera to view to the left side. I don't want that extra level of complexity now. 
4. Possibly not a suprise. I noticed a huge slow down in the development when I started to use 3D. This is because the laptop I used for this course is older and cheap so it can't handle the 3D very well. This is another reason besides complexity, why this section is extra.
